Work at DeHub
DeHub is on a mission to build the blockchain’s biggest and best decentralized entertainment ecosystem. We are a young, dynamic team of crypto and blockchain enthusiasts and professionals. We expect to be in growth mode for some time, and we are looking to recruit the brightest young things that share our vision and have talent to spare. Our audience is global, and our team is too; remote workers from anywhere with a stable internet connection are welcome.

If you are interested in joining us, check out the open positions below. If you think you have something extra to offer that we haven’t identified yet, don’t be afraid to get in touch and let us know.
  Senior Frontend Software Engineer (Angular)
Remote Position
  • At least 4+ years of recent working experience with Angular.
  • Experience with web3.js, ethers.js libraries.
  • Experience in building UIs and d'Apps interacting with the blockchain smart contracts via ABI.
  • Experience and passion in other Web3 APIs and libraries such as Walletconnect, Metamask, Trustwallet, Moralis, etc.
  • Basic knowledge of Solidity and Hardhat is a big plus.
  • Attention to detail and uncompromising focus on the excellent user experience.
We are aiming to build a world-class Web3 decentralized ecosystem of products and d'Apps, so UX and UI must be on par with the large legacy competitors.

You will be at the core of the fast-moving product development.
No legacy code, no corporate BS, just love for code and product development.
You will have a lot of freedom working directly with our CTO making technical decisions and building Angular components that interact with the blockchain and Moralis backend from scratch.

Introduce yourself at: tech@dehub.net
