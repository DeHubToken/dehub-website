Wallet Distribution

Operations wallets
The DeHub Marketing, R&D, Partnerships & Licensing Wallets are considered as operations wallets used for funding ongoing marketing campaigns, project development and DeHub originals, partnerships and the salaries of in-house employees.
Operation wallets cannot sell below their % allocation and only the reflections will be tradable.
Team wallet
The DeHub Team Wallet contains funds reserved for founders who initially funded and started the project.
Team's wallet cannot sell below their % allocation and only the reflections will be tradable.
Liquidity Pool (Pancakeswap listing)
Pancakeswap listing will be at a rate of 1 BNB = 200,000 DeHub
Private & public presales
A private sale will be hosted on our website at a rate of 1 BNB = 270,000 DeHub.
A public presale will be hosted on a reputable launchpad at a rate of 1 BNB = 235,000 DeHub.
Burnt Wallet
The DeHub Burned Wallet will receive its share from the 1% reflections in a process that slowly removes tokens from circulation thus increasing the value of the remaining tokens.

$DeHub "Trikenomics"

The $DeHub Protocol charges 12% transaction fees and it is distributed to 3 main features: token reflections to all holders, buy-back (we call it the Robin Hood System) and crypto collateral asset rewards to be claimed by holders from our rewards D’app.
2%
$DeHub Distribution
1% $DeHub reflections to all holders including burnt wallet
The other 1% will be reflecting to operations Wallets.
4%
Buy-Back
Robin Hood, our buy-back system uses the collected tax to buy back DeHub tokens, then instantly burns them which increases the token's value.
4%
BNB Distribution
2% To be claimed by holders from our rewards D'app & the other 2% to be utilized as project reserves.
2%
Liquidity Pool
2% of every transaction contributes toward automatically generating further liquidity on Pancake Swap.
