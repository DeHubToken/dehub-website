$DEHUB "Trikenomics"

The $DEHUB protocol charges 12% transaction fees and distributes them to 3 main features: token reflections to all holders, buy-back (we call it the Robin Hood System) and crypto collateral asset rewards to be claimed by holders from our rewards d’app.
2%
$DEHUB Distribution
1% $DEHUB reflections to all holders including burned wallet. The other 1% reflect to operations wallets.
4%
Buy-Back
Robin Hood, our buy-back system uses the collected tax to buy back $DEHUB tokens, then instantly burns them which increases the token's value.
4%
BNB Distribution
2% To be claimed by holders from our rewards d'app & the other 2% to be utilized as project reserves.
2%
Liquidity Pool
2% of every transaction contributes toward automatically generating further liquidity on Pancake Swap.

Measures
